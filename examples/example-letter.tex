\opening{Dear Prof.\ Carter,}

Thank you for the constructive feedback on our manuscript entitled
\textit{``RBANS Normative Data for Irish Adults Aged 50--90: Development,
Validation, and Clinical Utility''} (ACN-2025-0142). We are pleased to
submit a revised version that addresses each of the reviewers' comments.

A point-by-point response document is enclosed. In brief, the principal
changes are as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item We have added stratified norms by education level, as requested
        by Reviewer~1.
  \item The mediation analysis now includes bias-corrected bootstrap
        confidence intervals (5,000 resamples), per Reviewer~2's
        suggestion.
  \item We have expanded the Limitations section to acknowledge the
        single-site recruitment design.
\end{enumerate}

We believe these revisions have strengthened the manuscript substantially
and hope that it is now suitable for publication in
\textit{Archives of Clinical Neuropsychology}.

Please do not hesitate to contact me should you require any further
information or clarification.
